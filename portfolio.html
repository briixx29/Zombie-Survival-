About The Game

Zombie Survival is an intense, action-packed 2D top-down shooter built with Pygame where your only goal is to stay alive. Set in a post-apocalyptic world overrun by the undead, you must navigate through treacherous environments, fend off endless waves of zombies, and test your reflexes.

Featuring an immersive visual style, dynamic combat mechanics, and a variety of power-ups and weapons, the game is designed to keep you on the edge of your seat. Do you have what it takes to survive?

Endless Hordes

Survive against ever-growing waves of relentless zombies. The longer you last, the harder it gets.

Intense Combat

Master your aim and movement to dodge and destroy enemies in fluid top-down combat.

Power-ups

Collect guns, shields, speed boosts, and the special E-Jeep to turn the tide of battle.

Dynamic Stages

Progress through 3 unique environments, use portals to advance, and defeat a challenging final Boss.

Character Select

Choose from 7 different playable characters with their own unique avatars before dropping in.

Endless Mode

Beat the boss to unlock Endless Mode, and compete to achieve the highest survival time.

How to Play

W A S D

Movement

Use the W, A, S, D keys to move your character around the map.

Auto Aim & Attack

Shooting is automatic! Your character will auto-aim and continuously fire bullets at enemies.

ESC

Pause Menu

Press ESC to pause the game and access settings or quit.

Collectibles

Walk over items to pick up health, weapons, or protective shields.
